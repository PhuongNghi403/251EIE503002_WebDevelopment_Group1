About us

Founded in 2025, Pawfect Care was born from one simple belief: Pets are family.

We wanted to create a safe, playful, and loving space where every tail wag and every soft purr is cared for. From cozy homestays to fun spa days and thoughtful pet products, our mission is to make every pet’s day pawsome!

Caring and Consulting

Each pet is unique, with their own needs in health, behavior, and daily care. That’s why our services are designed not only to pamper, but also to protect and support.
